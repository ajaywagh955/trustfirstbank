Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I open an account with TrustFirst Bank ?

    Opening an account with TrustFirst Bank is easy. You can visit our nearest branch or apply online through our website.

  • What documents are required to apply for a loan ?

    The documentation requirements may vary depending on the type of loan. Generally, you will need proof of identification, income verification, and collateral details if applicable.

  • Can I access my accounts online ?

    Yes, TrustFirst Bank offers secure online banking services, allowing you to access and manage your accounts conveniently from anywhere, at any time.

  • How can I reset my online banking password ?

    You can easily reset your online banking password by following the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Alternatively, you can contact our customer support for assistance.

  • What are the fees associated with TrustFirst Bank accounts?

    The fees may vary depending on the type of account and services you choose. You can refer to our fee schedule or contact our customer service representatives for detailed information.

  • How can I report a lost or stolen debit/credit card ?

    If your card is lost or stolen, please contact our 24/7 customer service helpline immediately. We will assist you in blocking the card and arranging a replacement.

  • Is my money insured with TrustFirst Bank ?

    Yes, TrustFirst Bank is a member of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC), which provides deposit insurance coverage up to the maximum allowed by law.
